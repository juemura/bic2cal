About the E-mission App

E-mission's goal is to enable personal and structural shifts to sustainable transportation through a smartphone application that collects mobility data and provides users with insights about their travel patterns.

More information

Features

Track your travel patterns

Don't worry about having to manually input data. Your trips will be recorded automatically.

Play Habitica

when you engage in sustainable transportation, your avatar automatically gets points and rewards, which you can use to buy equipment, feed your pets, and fight monsters with friends!

Help build better cities

With the aggregate data that we collect, city planners can plan and implement improvements faster and cheaper.

Dashboard

Get insights into your transportation patterns and learn more about how you can improve. The dashboard displays a summary of your data for the past 7 days.

Different ways to look at your data

More of a graph person? Look at both your individual and the aggregate data using our metrics. You can filter by date, transportation mode, and metric (number of trips, distance, median speed, and trip duration).

Tour Model

You can use this tool to plan your commute, or simply to learn more about your travel patterns. In addition to the map, you'll find lots of information about your most common trips and places.
